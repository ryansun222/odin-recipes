Lasagna

A very popular lasagna recipe submitted to all recipe by John Chandler. All credit goes to John Chandler

Ingredients

  • meat: sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef
  • onions and garlic
  • tomato products
  • sugar
  • lasgna noodles
  • cheeses
  • egg

Steps

  1. make meat sauce
  2. cook the noodles
  3. make the ricotta mixture
  4. layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. cover with foil and bake
  6. let the lasagna rest before serving
